Our Products

We've spent many years buildign a set of products that can quickly deliver value to your organisation

AI Underwriting

Our AI underwriting tool uses the latest machine learning algorithms to quantify the risk of a new or existing policyholder. We cater for credit, motor and fleet insurance.

Intelligent OCR

Our Intelligent OCR tool uses deep learning and data mining techniques to extract and store the data from financial and bank statements, and eliminate all manual capturing of data.

Automated Capital Modeling

Our capital modeling engine will simulate losses based on probability distributions to give you the capital required to ensure solvency on various levels. We cater for credit insurance (trade credit and surety guarantees).
